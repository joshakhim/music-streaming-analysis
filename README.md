# 🎧 Music Streaming Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project explores a cleaned music streaming dataset to uncover trends in artist performance, platform distribution, audience engagement, and revenue generation.

The goal is to derive actionable insights that can help artists, record labels, and streaming platforms make data-driven decisions.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset contains streaming data across multiple platforms and countries.

### Features:
- **artist** – Name of the artist  
- **genre** – Music genre  
- **platform** – Streaming platform (e.g., Spotify, Audiomack, Apple Music)  
- **country** – Country of streams  
- **streams** – Total number of streams  
- **revenue_usd** – Revenue generated (USD)  
- **followers** – Artist follower count  
- **date** – Streaming date  

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze streaming distribution across platforms
- Identify top-performing artists and genres
- Examine revenue patterns and monetization efficiency
- Understand geographic trends in music consumption
- Explore the relationship between followers and streams

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 1. Streaming Performance
- Average streams per record: **~507K**
- Highest streams recorded: **~997K**
- Indicates strong variability in artist reach

### 2. Revenue Trends
- Average revenue: **~$2,537**
- Revenue does not scale linearly with streams, suggesting:
  - Platform payout differences
  - Monetization inefficiencies

### 3. Platform Distribution
- Platforms like **SoundCloud and Spotify** appear frequently
- Some platforms generate more streams but less revenue

### 4. Geographic Insights
- **Nigeria** appears as the most frequent country in the dataset
- Indicates strong engagement in emerging music markets

### 5. Genre Popularity
- **R&B** is the most common genre in the dataset
- Suggests higher production or popularity within this category

### 6. Audience Engagement
- Average followers: **~248K**
- Weak direct correlation between followers and streams:
  - Some artists with fewer followers achieve high streams
